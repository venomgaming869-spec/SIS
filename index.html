About SIS

Student Innovator Society (SIS) is a student-driven technical community at HRIT University focused on innovation, real-world problem solving, leadership development and hands-on technical learning.

We organize workshops, hackathons, bootcamps, industry sessions and collaborative projects to bridge the gap between academics and industry.
